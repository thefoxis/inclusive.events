All Inclusive

A guide for running diverse and inclusive events.

There’s an undeniable need for diversity of thought, but more importantly—the fundamental human right to equality. All Inclusive aims to be a handbook for inclusive, safe gatherings, operating under the principle of everyone’s welcome. 

Preorder

Who is this book for?

  • Anyone involved in organising events, from meetups to large scale conferences
  • Event and community managers
  • Volunteers
  • Individuals interested in diversity and inclusion (you!)

Topics include

  • Diversity

    Defining and acting on diversity goals.

  • Ticketing

    Inclusive ticket pricing, scholarships and aid for underrepresented groups.

  • Code of Conduct

    Writing, displaying and rule enforcement.

  • Call for Papers

    Speaker selection processes.

  • Accessibility and experience

    Choosing a venue, catering, childcare, providing transcription and interpretation.

  • Improvements

    Post-event debriefing, collecting feedback and working on improvements.

Become a patron

Self-publishing a book is a challenging endeavour. To ensure high quality of the publication, I’m looking for financial backing to cover the costs of hiring an editor, an illustrator and offering renumeration to individuals I’m planning to interview. My goals for diversity and producing a possible print edition will also present financial challenges.

The book will come with 10 limited patron spots for organisations—people who believe in and demonstrate their investment in diversity and inclusion on a internal level and beyond.

How does it work?

As a patron you’ll get:

  • An organisation name, website and a 200-word blurb placed at the back of the book.
  • A copy of the book.
  • Organisation’s logo on the site highlighting your support for diversity and inclusion causes.
  • A heartfelt thank you on social media and a blog post describing your organisation’s committment to diversity and inclusion.

If you want to back the project reach out to get more information.

Frequently Asked Questions

What’s the release date?

The book will go out in Q3 2016.

What formats will it be available in?

The first edition will be published as an eBook in Mobi, ePub and PDF.

I think this is important—What can I do to support the project?

You can spread the word about the book on Twitter, Facebook or share directly with your friends. When sharing use #beallinclusive hashtag. Alternatively, back the project financially by preordering a copy or becoming a patron.

Have more questions? Don’t hesitate to reach out.

About the author

Karolina Szczur

Karolina Szczur is a designer, developer and photographer from Krakow, Poland. She ran, spoke at and attended numerous events such as JSConf EU, CSSConf Oakland, CSSConf AU, RealtimeConf, and &yetConf. All Inclusive stems from her passion for user-centered experiences, empathetic design and frustration over the exclusive status quo.
